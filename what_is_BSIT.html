What is BSIT?

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

Discover the World of IT

BSIT is a fouryear degree focused on the practical and theoretical aspects of computing, technology, and systems management.

With BSIT, you gain the skills to design, develop, and maintain software, databases, networks, and IT infrastructures across industries.

Key Areas of Study

Programming & Software Development
Networking & System Administration
Cybersecurity & Data Protection
Web & Mobile Development
Database Management
IT Project Management
